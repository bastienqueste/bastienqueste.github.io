Congratulations to Estel Font on her doctoral thesis defence!

Estel defended her thesis, **"Ventilation of the Arabian Sea Oxygen Minimum Zone"**, in Natural Science with specialization in Oceanography at the University of Gothenburg.

The defence took place on Thursday 16 October 2025 at 13:00 in Hall 3401 Korallrevet, Natrium, Medicinaregatan 7B, Gothenburg.

The faculty opponent was Dr. Marina Lévy from LOCEAN, Sorbonne University. The examination board consisted of Dr. Johannes Karstensen, Professor Eleanor Frajka-Williams, and Professor Isaac Rodrigues Dos Santos, with Professor Fabien Roquet chairing the public defence.

Congratulations Estel!
